Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 25, 2025

"OurCloud" is a mobile application created to help couples store and share their photos and memories in a private and secure way. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Google Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we receive basic profile information such as:

  • Your Google account ID
  • Your email address
  • Your display name

This information is used only to identify your account within the app.

1.2 Google Drive Access

The App uses the following Google Drive scope:

• drive.file – “See, edit, create, and delete only the specific Google Drive files you use with this app.”

With this scope, the App can:

  • Upload photos or files to a folder in your own Google Drive
  • View and manage only the files and folders created by the App
  • Access files only within the App's created directory

The App cannot access, view, or modify any other files in your Google Drive.

1.3 Uploaded Photos and Files

Photos and files you choose to store are uploaded directly to your Google Drive. They are not stored on our servers, because we do not operate any servers. Only you (and your partner if you share the folder) can access them through Google Drive.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only for the core features of the App:

  • Authenticating your Google account
  • Uploading and managing photos/files in your chosen Google Drive folder
  • Allowing you to share a Drive folder with your partner
  • Displaying your uploaded memories within the App

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Store any of your photos or files on external servers

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All photos and files are stored directly in your own Google Drive.
  • We do not store, process, or back up your files on our servers.
  • All authentication is handled securely through Google OAuth.
  • All communication between your device and Google APIs is encrypted.

4. Shared Access

If you choose to share your Google Drive folder with your partner, the visibility of your photos and files is controlled entirely by Google Drive’s sharing settings. The App does not have access to anything outside this shared folder.

5. Your Choices

  • You can revoke the App’s Google Drive access at any time from Google Account → Security → Third-party access.
  • You may delete any files directly from Google Drive.
  • You may uninstall the App at any time.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes occur, the updated version will be posted here.
